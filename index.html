Here is a little of my story

I was born in Michigan and grew up in San Diego, California. I first came to the Bay Area in 2010 when I attended Santa Clara University studying Mechanical Engineering and playing on the Division 1 Water Polo team.

2015-2017

Mechanical Design Engineer

Starting my first career out of college I was designing HVAC systems for commertial buildings. Working with project management to understand project requirements and using CAD software to create systems that met clients needs and city/state code requirements.

2017-2019

Project Engineer

During my time as a Project Engineer I worked directly with Project Managers to assist in project management duties for multiple projects at a time. Key responsabilities included creating proposals for new proejcts, estimating project costs for proposal budgets and meticuous communication with internal team leadership and external project team members.

2019

Senior Project Engineer

As a Senior Project Engineer I ran projects from 100K to over 4 Million in revenue with oversight from the Project Executive team. I worked as the main point of contact for all project related work including scheduling of personal and project deadlines, equipment design and procurement and approval of design engineering drawings.

2019-2020

App Academy

This 16-week intensive coding bootcamp covered a Full Stack curriculum and had an acceptance rate of < 3%. The major technologies and languages learned were Ruby on Rails, JavaScript and React/Redux. Other topics covered include: Relational(PostgreSQL) and Non Relational(MongoDB) databases, jQuery, MERN stack and software engineering fundamentals. With such a condensed program, I spent 90-100 hours per week throughout the duration of the course.

2020

Hacker in Residence

Hacker in Residence duties inlcude conducting technical screening for applicants, evaluating adeptness in JavaScript, Python and Ruby. As well as performing non-technical interviews to gauge applicants alignment with App Academy values.

2020

Freelance/Open Source

After my time at App Academy I have been futher expanding my knowledge of Software Engineering, learning Python, docker and graphQL. I have continued creating side projects focused on learning new librarys/tools/languages/frameworks. Most recently my focus is breaking into the Open Source Contributing. I believe the software engineering community is an amazing place where so many people are here to help others and what better way to learn than contribute to Open Source projects.
